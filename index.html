4 · Why custom theme + plugin

Why not a template, page builder, or SaaS?

It’s a fair question — those are cheaper up front. Here is why a custom build is the right call for a booking business, and where the money actually goes.

Booking logic no plugin sells

Pricing here is per-cabin and per-person, with occupancy sharing, weekday discounts, holiday rates and child pricing (free / half / full). Off-the-shelf booking plugins can’t model six shared cabins on one boat — this engine was written for exactly that.

Speed you can’t retrofit

Page builders ship heavy, generic code that drags load time — and slow sites lose mobile bookings. This build is lean, single-stylesheet, no jQuery, with images optimised ~74% smaller. Fast by construction, not by plugin patch.

No monthly rent

SaaS builders and premium booking add-ons charge every month, forever. A custom build is paid once and owned. Over a couple of years that is the difference between an expense and an asset.

Bangla-first, brand-first

The whole experience is written in Bangla for local guests, in this brand’s voice and colours. A template forces the business to fit the tool; a custom build fits the tool to the business.

Security & data control

Bookings are stored privately, invoices use signed private links, and every form is protected against spam and abuse. Guest data lives on the client’s own site — not on a third-party platform.

Extensible & future-proof

Clean, documented WordPress code means any developer can extend it — add server-side conversion tracking, new pages, payment gateways — without being trapped in a proprietary builder.

5 · Engineering quality

Built to professional standards

The parts a guest never sees are what keep the site fast, safe, and reliable.

⚡

Performance

Single stylesheet, no jQuery, lazy-loaded images, font preconnect, and a one-time image optimisation that cut theme image weight by about 74% — faster loads mean more completed mobile bookings.

🔍

SEO & discoverability

Per-page meta descriptions, Open Graph & Twitter cards, a connected JSON-LD knowledge graph (Organization, LocalBusiness, reviews, breadcrumbs), XML sitemap and correct language signals.

🔒

Security

Nonce-verified forms, capability checks, honeypot & rate-limiting against spam, private booking records with no public API exposure, and timing-safe signed invoice links.

📱

Responsive & accessible

Mobile-first layout, a dedicated mobile action bar, readable Bangla typography, and reduced-motion support — a clean experience on any device.

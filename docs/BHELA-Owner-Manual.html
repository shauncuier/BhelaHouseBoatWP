01 Logging in

Everything is managed from the WordPress dashboard — the control panel behind your website.

  1. Open yourdomain.com/wp-admin in your browser.
  2. Enter your username and password. (Keep these private — anyone with them can change the site.)
  3. You land on the Dashboard. On the left is the menu you'll use for everything below.
💡

The one menu you'll use most is 🛶 Bookings. Its first item, 📊 Dashboard, is your control centre — booking counts, money in, upcoming trips, recent activity, a setup checklist, and one-click buttons for every task. Open it first each day. The trip calendar, gallery, reviews, settings and guide all live in the same menu.

02 Managing bookings

Every booking request from the website is saved automatically. You never lose a lead.

Go to Bookings → All Bookings. Each row is one request, showing the guest name, invoice number, travel date, and status. Click a booking to open it.

Inside a booking you can:

  • See the guest's name, phone, email, travel date, cabins, guests, and price.
  • Record how much they've paid and the payment method / transaction ID.
  • Change the status as the booking progresses (next section).
  • Open the guest's invoice.
➕

You can also add a booking yourself (phone/walk-in guests): Bookings → Add New Booking.

03 Booking status & notifications

The status tells everyone where a booking stands. Changing it can automatically message the guest.

StatusMeaning — use it when…
PendingA new request just came in. Nothing paid yet.
Advance PaidGuest paid the 50% advance. Seat held.
ConfirmedBooking is locked in. This sends the guest a confirmation.
CompletedTrip is over.
CancelledBooking was called off.

What happens automatically

  • When you set a booking to Confirmed, the guest gets a confirmation email (if they gave an email).
  • If SMS is turned on, changing the status also texts the guest the new status.
  • You (the owner) get an email + SMS the moment any new booking arrives.
✅

You control exactly which of these fire — see Email notifications and SMS notifications.

04 Trip calendar & cabin availability

Set your departure dates and how many of the 6 cabins are already booked — the website updates itself.

Go to Bookings → Trip Calendar and press Add Trip for a new row. For every trip you set:

ColumnWhat to put
Start DateThe trip's departure date.
End DateLeave blank for a standard 2-day 1-night trip. For a Full Boat or longer charter, set the real return date.
Display LabelFills in automatically from the dates (e.g. 7 – 8 Aug 2026) — override only if you want.
DaysBangla day names, fill in automatically (e.g. শুক্র – শনি).
ছুটিTick if this departure is a holiday. It then charges the Regular rate (no 20% weekday discount) and shows "ছুটির দিন" on the site.
StatusAvailable / Filling Fast / Booked.
Booked CabinsHow many of the 6 cabins are already taken (0–6).
DeleteTick to remove the row, then Save.

Fill the Start Date and the label, days and duration set themselves. Press Save and the website updates instantly.

🛏️

Availability is now automatic: the moment a booking is Advance Paid or Confirmed, its cabins come out of that date's availability by themselves — you don't touch the calendar, and every manager plus the website sees the same live number. The Booked Cabins field is just a manual hold (minimum) — use it to block cabins for a phone booking or a full-boat charter. Set it to 6 for Full Booked.

Each row shows the live count — এখন বুকড x/6 · খালি y (অটো) — so you always see the real remaining cabins at a glance.

🎉

Holidays live here now. Ticking a trip's ছুটি box is the only place you set holiday pricing — there is no separate holiday list in Settings anymore.

05 Invoices

Every booking has a branded invoice with a private link — safe to send to the guest.

  • Open a booking → the invoice link is on the edit screen (and inside the confirmation email).
  • The invoice shows each cabin, the per-person rate, guests, total, advance, paid, and due.
  • Payment details (bKash, Nagad, bank, QR images) come from your Settings and appear on the invoice.
🔒

Each invoice link has a private key, so only someone with the link can open it. It's safe to share on WhatsApp or email.

06 Cabin rates & pricing days

Change prices, weekend days, and holidays yourself — no developer needed.

All in Bookings → Settings:

Cabin Rates

Each cabin has a Regular/Holiday rate and a cheaper Weekday rate, per person. Edit the numbers and Save.

Pricing Days

  • Weekend Days — tick which days charge the Regular rate (default: Friday, Saturday).
  • Every other day automatically uses the discounted Weekday rate.
🗓️

Holidays are no longer a list here. Mark a departure as a holiday by ticking its ছুটি box in the Trip Calendar — it then charges the Regular rate with no weekday discount.

⚠️

Children: 0–4 years free; 4–8 years pay a flat ৳5,000 each (no weekday discount); 9+ full rate — built in and applied automatically. The amount is editable at Settings → শিশু (৪–৮) ফি.

07 Email notifications

Control which booking emails go out, and where your own alerts land.

In Bookings → Settings → 📧 Email Notifications:

  • Enable emails — the master on/off switch.
  • Turn on/off each type: new booking → you, new booking → customer, confirmed → customer.
  • Owner notification email — where your new-booking alerts go (blank = your business email).
  • From name and Reply-To — how your emails appear to guests.
  • Send Test Email — check it works right now.
📮

Email delivery is handled by FluentSMTP (already set up). If emails ever stop, that's the plugin to check first.

08 SMS notifications

Optional — text the guest and yourself automatically. Works with any Bangladesh SMS gateway.

In Bookings → Settings → 📱 SMS Notifications. It's off by default. To switch it on:

  1. Buy an SMS package from a gateway (e.g. BulkSMSBD) and get your API key + Sender ID.
  2. Tick Enable SMS, choose BulkSMSBD as the gateway.
  3. Paste your API Key and Sender ID.
  4. Set the Admin SMS number (blank = Phone 1).
  5. Save, then press Send Test SMS to confirm.

You can edit the three Bangla message templates. Words in curly braces get filled in automatically — for example {name}, {invoice}, {date}, {total}, {due}, {status}.

🔁

Using a different provider? Pick Custom and paste that gateway's API URL and field names — no code change needed.

09 Guest reviews

The reviews on your homepage are managed like posts.

Go to Bookings → All Reviews → Add New. Put the guest's name as the Title, their words as the content, set a star rating and a trip type (e.g. "Family Trip · Dhaka"). Published reviews show on the site automatically.

10 Blog — হাওর জার্নাল

Publish travel guides and tips to attract visitors from Google. Three starter articles ship ready.

  1. Go to Posts → Add New.
  2. Write your title and content.
  3. On the right, pick a Category (ভ্রমণ গাইড / হাওরের খবর / টিপস) and add Tags.
  4. Set a Featured Image (the photo shown on the card).
  5. Press Publish.

The blog lives at yourdomain.com/blog. Category and tag pages, "related posts", reading time, and a booking call-to-action on every article are all automatic.

✍️

Comments are turned off on purpose (to avoid spam) — guests are guided to WhatsApp/booking instead.

11 Editing site text & images

Change your phone numbers, homepage words, and photos without touching code.

Go to Appearance → Customize. Five BHELA panels:

PanelWhat you can change
BHELA ContactPhone, WhatsApp, Messenger, email, address, and all social links — Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, YouTube, X, Threads (icons appear in the footer; blank = hidden).
BHELA HomepageThe big hero headline, badge, and subtitle on the front page.
BHELA ImagesSwap the hero, food, rooftop, cabin, and spot photos. Leave blank to keep the defaults.
BHELA TrackingPaste your Google Analytics ID (G-…) and Facebook Pixel ID — the tracking code is added automatically; your own admin visits are never counted.
BHELA Custom CodePaste any snippet into <head>, right after <body>, or before </body> — site-wide, no theme editing.

Cabin names, prices, and payment details are in Bookings → Settings (not the Customizer).

📞

Your phone/WhatsApp/email set in Bookings → Settings is the source of truth — the whole site (footer, buttons, invoice, emails) reads from there.

12 Gallery photos — গ্যালারি

Manage the photo gallery, and add many photos at once.

Photos live under Bookings → 🖼️ Gallery.

⚡

Add many at once (fastest): Bookings → 🖼️ Bulk Upload → ছবি বাছাই করুন → select a whole batch from the media library (drag new files in and pick them all) → optionally tick a category → যোগ করুন. Each photo becomes its own gallery item, added after the current last one.

Add one photo: 🖼️ Gallery → নতুন ছবি → set the Featured Image (the photo itself) → type a caption in the title box → tick a ক্যাটাগরি → Publish.

Categories become the filter tabs on the public gallery page; a tab appears once a category has at least one photo. The Order number controls sequence. First-time setup offers a one-click import of the theme's bundled photos.

13 Contact page — যোগাযোগ

A ready contact page with quick-contact cards and a form that emails you.

The যোগাযোগ page (/contact/) is created automatically and added to the menu. It shows quick-contact cards (phone, WhatsApp, Messenger, email), your address and hours, social icons, plus a contact form.

Form messages are emailed to you — the address from Bookings → Settings → Owner notification email (or your business email). Nothing is stored in the database, and you can reply directly to the guest (their email is set as Reply-To).

💬

To show the Messenger card, paste your m.me/yourpage link in Customize → BHELA Contact → Messenger link. Leave blank to hide that card.

14 Activity log — কী কী হচ্ছে

A plain record of what the plugin actually did — so you can answer "did that work?" without a developer.

Go to Bookings → Activity Log. Newest first, it records:

  • New bookings received, and emails / SMS sent or failed.
  • Status changes, settings saved, gallery imports and bulk uploads.
  • Every trip-calendar save, including which dates were removed — so a disappearing departure can always be traced.

Each line is ✅ (worked) or ❌ (problem). Filter by type with the buttons at the top; the latest 300 events are kept.

15 Troubleshooting

If this happens…Do this
Emails not arrivingCheck FluentSMTP is connected, and that Email Notifications is enabled. Use Send Test Email.
SMS not sendingConfirm your gateway has balance, the API key + Sender ID are correct, SMS is enabled. Use Send Test SMS.
A date shows wrong availabilityUpdate Booked Cabins for that trip in the Trip Calendar.
Prices look wrongCheck Cabin Rates and Weekend Days in Settings, and the trip's ছুটি holiday tick in the Trip Calendar.
Something looks broken after an updateRefresh the page fully (Ctrl+Shift+R). If it persists, contact your developer.
🛟

Golden rule: Settings, bookings, calendar, reviews, and blog posts are all safe to edit yourself. Avoid the Plugins, Users, and Theme File Editor screens — those are for your developer.
